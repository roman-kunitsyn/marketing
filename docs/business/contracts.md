# Contracts

This document defines the business rules that must be reflected in client agreements.

It is not legal advice. It is a documentation guide for scope, delivery, revision, and ownership rules.

How To Use:

Read this file before drafting proposals, statements of work, or delivery terms. Keep it aligned with [Pricing](pricing.md) and [Workflow](workflow.md).

## Contract Topics

- Scope of work
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Revision policy
- Approval policy
- Payment terms
- Ownership and usage rights
- Confidentiality
- Cancellation and offboarding

## Required Clauses

- Define which artifacts are included.
- Define who approves each stage.
- Define how many revision rounds are included.
- Define what counts as out-of-scope work.
- Define when delivery is considered complete.

## Contract Rules

- Contracts must match the service catalog.
- Pricing must match the agreed scope.
- Delivery terms must match the business workflow.
- If the scope changes, the contract must be updated before production continues.
