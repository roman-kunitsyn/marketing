# Business

This folder defines how the agency operates as a business.

It covers the operating model, service structure, pricing, client journey, contracts, and business workflow.
It is the source of truth for how the agency sells and delivers work.

Use this folder for:

- business model
- service catalog
- pricing
- client journey
- business workflow
- contracts
- deliverables
- revision policy
- onboarding and offboarding

Business knowledge stays separate from agency roles, marketing strategy, content planning, and execution.

How To Use:

Read [README](../README.md) first, then use this folder to understand how the agency operates as a business.
