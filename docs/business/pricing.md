# Pricing

This document defines how the agency prices work.

It should keep pricing simple enough for scoping, while still reflecting strategy, production effort, and approval overhead.

How To Use:

Read this file before preparing quotes or proposals. Use it together with [Service Catalog](service_catalog.md) and [Contracts](contracts.md).

## Pricing Models

- Fixed-scope project pricing
- Monthly retainer pricing
- Audit or strategy sprint pricing
- Add-on pricing for extra platforms, revisions, or expedited delivery

## Pricing Inputs

- Scope size
- Number of artifacts
- Number of platforms
- Revision allowance
- Turnaround time
- Research depth
- Review complexity

## Pricing Rules

- Price the full artifact chain, not isolated outputs.
- Separate strategy, production, and revision costs.
- Include approval and QA effort in the quote.
- Do not hide deliverables behind vague line items.

## Quote Components

- Discovery and onboarding
- Strategy artifacts
- Production artifacts
- Review cycle
- Delivery and handoff
