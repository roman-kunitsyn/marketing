# Workflow

This document defines the business workflow for the AI-First Marketing Agency.

It covers how work moves from lead qualification to delivery and renewal.

How To Use:

Read this file when implementing business operations, onboarding steps, or service handoffs.

## Workflow

1. Qualify the lead.
2. Confirm the offer and scope.
3. Collect client inputs.
4. Produce the [Client Interview](../schemas/client_interview.schema.json).
5. Produce the [Marketing Brief](../schemas/marketing_brief.schema.json).
6. Create strategy artifacts.
7. Produce production artifacts.
8. Run review and revision.
9. Deliver the approved package.
10. Close, renew, or expand the engagement.

## Workflow Rules

- Do not begin production before strategy is approved.
- Do not deliver without review and acceptance.
- Keep the business workflow aligned with the artifact flow in [Project](../PROJECT.md).
- Each step must have a clear owner and next handoff.

## Operational Outputs

- Client qualification status
- Approved scope
- Approved artifact chain
- Delivery confirmation
- Renewal decision
