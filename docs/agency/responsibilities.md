# Responsibilities

This document defines what each agency role is responsible for beyond raw artifact ownership.

It helps agents and humans distinguish between producing an artifact and being accountable for its quality and handoff.

How To Use:

Read this file when defining scope, delegating work, or reviewing who should act next in the workflow.

## Marketing Strategist

- Collect client input
- Translate requirements into strategy
- Confirm scope and campaign direction

## Content Strategist

- Turn strategy into a content plan
- Define content structure and sequencing
- Ensure the package fits the campaign goals

## Copywriter

- Write all copy assets
- Maintain tone, clarity, and CTA consistency
- Adapt message to the target platform and content type

## Creative Designer

- Define visual direction
- Produce visual concepts and creative instructions
- Keep creative output aligned with brand and platform constraints

## Prompt Builder

- Convert creative needs into image prompts
- Preserve visual intent and generation constraints
- Keep prompts reusable and schema-aligned

## Social Media Manager

- Prepare publishing-ready output
- Map assets to platforms and schedule
- Capture metadata needed for delivery

## QA Reviewer

- Validate completeness
- Check consistency across artifacts
- Confirm schema, naming, and content quality before delivery

## Responsibility Rules

- Responsibility includes review of the produced artifact, not just generation.
- If an artifact is incomplete, the producing role must fix it before handoff.
- QA can reject any artifact that breaks the canonical flow or schema rules.
