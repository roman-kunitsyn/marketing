# Content Pillars

This document defines the recurring themes used to structure content.

Content pillars help the agency keep campaigns focused, balanced, and easy to expand.

How To Use:

Read this file when building campaign strategy or content strategy artifacts.

## Pillar Criteria

- Relevant to the audience
- Relevant to the business goal
- Durable enough to reuse
- Distinct enough to avoid repetition

## Common Pillar Types

- Education
- Authority
- Proof
- Offer
- Community
- Brand story
- Conversion

## Rules

- Content pillars should be defined before production starts.
- A campaign should not rely on a single pillar unless intentionally narrow.
- Pillars should map to content packages and platform adaptation.
