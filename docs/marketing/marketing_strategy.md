# Marketing Strategy

This document defines the marketing strategy layer used by the agency.

It explains how business goals are translated into campaign direction, audience choices, messaging, and content priorities.

How To Use:

Read this file before creating campaign strategy or content strategy artifacts. Use it with [Business](../business/DESCRIPTION.md), [Agency](../agency/DESCRIPTION.md), and [Schemas](../schemas/DESCRIPTION.md).

## Purpose

- Define the marketing intent behind a campaign.
- Keep strategy separate from production and publishing.
- Provide a stable reference for downstream artifacts.

## Strategy Inputs

- Business goals
- Client interview
- Brand profile
- Target audience
- Platform scope
- Campaign type

## Strategy Outputs

- Marketing brief
- Campaign strategy
- Content strategy
- Messaging direction
- Audience framing

## Strategy Rules

- Marketing strategy must remain platform-aware but platform-independent in principle.
- Strategy should be reusable across campaign variations.
- Naming should match the canonical terms used in [Project](../PROJECT.md) and [Architecture](../ARCHITECTURE.md).
