# Marketing Goals

This document defines the canonical marketing goals used by the agency.

Goals are the measurable outcomes that guide strategy and content decisions.

How To Use:

Read this file when defining the objective of a campaign or aligning content to business outcomes.

## Goal Types

- Awareness
- Reach
- Engagement
- Traffic
- Leads
- Conversions
- Retention
- Upsell

## Goal Rules

- Every campaign should map to one primary goal.
- Secondary goals should not conflict with the primary goal.
- Goal wording should be consistent across briefs, strategies, and review artifacts.
