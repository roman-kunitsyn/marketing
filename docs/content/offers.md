# Offers

This document defines the offer content type.

Offer content presents a service, product, or promotion with a clear action.

How To Use:

Read this file when planning conversion-oriented content.

## Offer Purpose

- Present a clear value proposition
- Drive action
- Support sales or lead generation

## Offer Rules

- The offer should match the business model and pricing.
- The call to action should be explicit.
- The offer should stay aligned with the approved campaign strategy.
