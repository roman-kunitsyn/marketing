# Campaign Creation Workflow

This workflow defines how the campaign planning stage is completed.

It converts the client interview into marketing brief and campaign strategy artifacts.

How To Use:

Read this file when implementing the strategy handoff from business intake to campaign planning.

## Inputs

- `client_interview.json`
- Brand profile
- Campaign type
- Marketing goals

## Steps

1. Review the interview for completeness.
2. Produce `marketing_brief.json`.
3. Produce `campaign_strategy.json`.
4. Validate scope and platform fit.

## Outputs

- `marketing_brief.json`
- `campaign_strategy.json`

## Rules

- One primary owner per artifact.
- The campaign strategy must be grounded in the approved brief.
- Do not move forward with unresolved scope questions.
